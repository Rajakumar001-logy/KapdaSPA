How VASTRA CARE works

The same convenience playbook as modern food delivery—applied to garment care, with clear timelines and careful handling.

  1. 01

    Book a slot

    Choose services, add notes for delicates, and pick a pickup window that works for you.

  2. 02

    We collect

    Our team tags your bag, confirms items, and brings everything to our vetted facility.

  3. 03

    Expert care

    Wash & fold, dry cleaning, pressing—each load processed to fabric-safe standards.

  4. 04

    Fresh delivery

    Track status in the app and get clothes back neatly packed, on time.
